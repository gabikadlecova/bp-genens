\section{Machine learning}
The field of Machine learning encompasses a broad range of algorithms and statistical methods for data processing. In his book on Machine learning, Flach provides the following general definition: \textquote{Machine learning is the systematic study of algorithms and systems that improve their knowledge or performance with experience.} (\citet{Flach:2012:MLA:2490546})
